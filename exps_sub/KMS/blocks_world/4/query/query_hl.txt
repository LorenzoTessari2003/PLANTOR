There are 3 blocks on the table in positions (1,1), (2,3) and (3,4) respectively. The goal is to put
the first in position (5,5), put the second on top of it and the third on top of the second.
There is only one agent that can do this operation. It is available at the beginning and will be 
available at the end. Moreover, it can:
- Move a block from a position on the table to another position in the table
- Move a block that is on the table to the top of another block
- Move a block on top of a block to another position on the table
- Move a block on top of a block to the top of another block
