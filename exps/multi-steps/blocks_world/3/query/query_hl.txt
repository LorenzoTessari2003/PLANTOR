There are 4 blocks on a table. In a first time the block A is in position (1,1) block B is on top of block A, C on top of B and D on top of C. After some rearrangements A is in the same position, B is on the table in position (2,1), also C is on the table in position (3,1) and D is still on top of A.
There are 2 agents  with a robotic arm, they can:
- Move a block from a position to another position in the table
- Move a block that is on the table to the top of another one 
- Move a block on top of a block to another position
